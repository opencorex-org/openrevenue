# Security policy

Do not open public issues for vulnerabilities. Email the OpenCorex team at `info.opencorex@gmail.com` with the subject `OpenRevenue security report` and include the affected version, impact, reproduction, and suggested remediation. Maintainers will acknowledge reports within five business days, coordinate validation and remediation, and credit reporters who request it. Never include real taxpayer data, credentials, or exploitable production details in reports or fixtures. Supported versions will be listed here after the first release.
