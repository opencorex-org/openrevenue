# Code of Conduct

We follow the [Contributor Covenant 2.1](https://www.contributor-covenant.org/version/2/1/code_of_conduct/). Be respectful, inclusive, and constructive. Harassment, disclosure of private data, and abuse are unacceptable. Report conduct concerns privately to the OpenCorex team at `info.opencorex@gmail.com` with the subject `OpenRevenue conduct report`; maintainers will respond proportionately and confidentially.
